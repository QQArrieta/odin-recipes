Crab Rangoon

Description

Crab Rangoon contains a simple mixture of crab, cream cheese, and seasonings wrapped in a wonton wrapper and fried crispy (or baked in the oven)! This easy recipe is a go-to at any Chinese restaurant but it is also super easy to make at home! We love to dip our Crab Rangoon in a simple sweet and sour dip (just like our fave restaurant) for maximum flavor!
